Leave Policy and Quota Guidelines

1. Annual Leave: 20 days per year for all full-time employees
2. Sick Leave: 10 days per year with medical documentation required
3. Maternity Leave: 12 weeks paid leave for new mothers
4. Paternity Leave: 2 weeks paid leave for new fathers
5. Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family members
6. Unpaid Leave: Available for up to 3 months with management approval
7. Leave Carry Forward: Maximum 5 days can be carried forward to next year
8. Leave Application: Must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
9. Emergency Leave: Available for unforeseen circumstances with immediate supervisor approval
10. Leave Balance: Can be checked through the HR portal at any time
